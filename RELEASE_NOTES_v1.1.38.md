# Recipe Helper v1.1.38

## Highlights

- Save Direct Ingredients and Raw Materials as independent item snapshots, then load and use them without a recipe selected.
- Keep these independent plans through updates, duplicates, imports, exports, combined loads, and the Missing Items Overlay.
- Hover saved folders to browse immediate subfolders or their direct recipe contents; long recipe lists use two columns.
- Rounded main sections, saved-plan folders, and table headers for a more consistent layout.

## Overlay improvements

- Transparent overlay mode keeps Missing and live timer cards readable with dark, lightly tinted backgrounds.
- Always Up availability cards retain the lighter transparent presentation.
