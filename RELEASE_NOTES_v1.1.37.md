# Recipe Helper v1.1.37

## Highlights

- Find aetherial-reduction results such as Levinchrome Aethersand directly in search, with hover details showing their reduction sources.
- Save, load, update, duplicate, move, export, and import plans containing recipes, gatherables, and collectables.
- Improve saved-plan controls with separate Save new plan and Update plan actions, safer folder management, clear selection controls, and calmer collapsed startup sections.
- Improve hover presentation with rounded themed outlines, a more reliable crafting-log display, and Master Recipe exclusions where FFXIV does not record completion.

## Notes

- Craft actions now clearly disable when required materials are not in live inventory and explain why on hover.
- The local development copy is moved aside during the release process so it cannot override the installed release.
