# Recipe Helper v1.1.42

## Log Status Filter

- Added All Log Statuses, Obtained in Log, and Not Obtained in Log filters.
- The filter uses Gathering, Fishing, Spearfishing, and confirmed Crafting Log progress.
- Quest items, society quest items, Cosmic Exploration items, special-tool items, and ephemeral nodes are excluded because they do not have reliable ordinary log progress.

## Cosmic Exploration

- Cosmic Exploration detection now covers all WKS items, including gathering items such as Amber Pseudostone.
- Cosmic Exploration items no longer show ordinary Gathering Log rows.
- Duplicate Gathering Log rows are now collapsed for normal items.
