# Recipe Helper v1.1.48

## Artisan crafting queue

- Consolidates shared pre-crafts into one batch and completes all pre-crafts before final recipes begin.
- Moves completed pre-crafts and recipes into a collapsible Completed section, keeping the active queue focused on the remaining work.
- Adds an in-cell green crafting-progress fill. Hover a status cell to see its exact craft count.
