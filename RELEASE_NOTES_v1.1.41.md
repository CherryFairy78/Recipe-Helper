# Recipe Helper v1.1.41

## New Filters

- Selecting a crafting job now offers a Master Recipe Book filter containing only the books relevant to that job.
- Selecting MIN, BTN, or FSH now offers a Folklore Book filter.
- Selecting FSH also offers a Fish Type filter for Regular Fish, Big Fish, Spearfishing, Ocean Fishing, and other GatherBuddy categories.

## Folklore And Folders

- Folklore hovers now show the actual tome name and prefer current Purple Gatherers' Scrip costs over obsolete Regional Folklore Trader's Tokens.
- Folklore Items now display as the Fieldcraft Items, Folklore Items exchange, including correct Purple Gatherers' Scrip costs.
- Folder Rename preserves a subfolder's parent, supports slashes in renamed labels, and prevents duplicate sibling names.
- Move folder pickers now show parent and subfolder relationships in an expanded indented tree.
